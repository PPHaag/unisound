# Unisound

## Concept

Unisound is a platform connecting musicians, DJs, bands, venues and event organizers.

The goal is to simplify the process of finding, booking and managing live music and event entertainment.

## Core components

• Artist discovery  
• Booking marketplace  
• Venue connections  
• Equipment rental connections  
• Event services integration

## Target users

- DJs
- Musicians
- Bands
- Event organizers
- Hospitality venues
- Wedding planners
- Corporate events

## Vision

Create a central platform where the supply and demand of live music and event entertainment meet in a transparent and efficient way.

## Strategic role

Unisound connects music culture, hospitality and events into one ecosystem.

The long-term potential lies in becoming a **music and event infrastructure platform** rather than only a booking tool.
